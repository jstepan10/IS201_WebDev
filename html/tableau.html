The following is a tableau visual analysis of profitability across the United States, trends in profitability in a highly profitable state, which product categories are the most profitable, and the forecast for how well those products will sell over the next two years. Across the United States, California and New York have the highest profitability, while Ohio and Texas have the lowest.




In California, profits have varied greatly month to month, but have shown a general upward trend over the past two years.




In California, the most profitable product category of office supplies, technology, and furniture is office supplies.




Based on past data, a forecast of units of office supplies sold in California for the next two years shows a continuing upward trend.
